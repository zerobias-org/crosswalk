# Changelog

All notable changes to the AIUC-1 to EU AI Act Crosswalk will be documented in this file.

## [1.0.0] - 2026-02-02

### Added
- Initial crosswalk mapping between AIUC-1 AI Agent Standard v1 and EU AI Act
- 60+ element mappings covering all 6 AIUC-1 domains
- Domain A (Data & Privacy) mapped to EU AI Act Articles 10, 13
- Domain B (Security) mapped to EU AI Act Article 15 (Cybersecurity)
- Domain C (Safety) mapped to EU AI Act Articles 9, 14, 15
- Domain D (Reliability) mapped to EU AI Act Articles 9, 15
- Domain E (Accountability) mapped to EU AI Act Articles 11, 12, 13, 14, 16, 17, 26
- Domain F (Society) mapped to EU AI Act Articles 9, 15
- Relationship strength indicators (5-9 scale) for each mapping
- Notes explaining the rationale for each mapping

### Notes
- This crosswalk requires the EU AI Act framework to be published for full platform integration
- Mappings focus on high-risk AI system requirements (Articles 6-15)
- Additional mappings may be added for general-purpose AI model requirements
